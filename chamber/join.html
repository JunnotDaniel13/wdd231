NP Membership Benefits

  • Complimentary chamber membership for verified non-profit entities.
  • Access to monthly collaboration roundtables and grant-writing workshops.
  • Spotlight listing in our community service newsletter once per year.
  • Guidance on navigating public and private funding opportunities.

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • Invitation to quarterly business development workshops.
  • Business directory listing with social media links.
  • Discounted vendor booth rates at chamber events.
  • Access to the members-only resource library.

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze benefits plus bi-monthly marketing clinics.
  • Featured article on the chamber home page once per year.
  • Priority referrals through our business concierge team.
  • Two complimentary tickets to signature networking events.

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver benefits plus quarterly strategic planning sessions.
  • Premium placement on the chamber website and newsletters.
  • Sponsored speaking opportunities at flagship chamber events.
  • Dedicated advocacy support for policy initiatives.
